Employee Onboarding Process

The onboarding process is designed to help new employees and interns integrate smoothly into the organization.

Pre-Arrival (Before First Day):
- Complete background check and paperwork
- Review welcome materials sent via email
- Prepare questions about your role
- Confirm start date and time with HR

Day 1 - Orientation:
- Welcome meeting with HR (9:00 AM)
- Company overview and culture introduction
- Benefits enrollment session
- IT setup and account creation
- Office tour and introductions
- Meet with your manager and team

Week 1 - Integration:
- Complete required training modules
- Review job responsibilities with manager
- Set up workspace and tools
- Attend department meetings
- Begin working on initial assignments
- Schedule regular check-ins with manager

Month 1 - Settling In:
- Complete all mandatory training
- Participate in team projects
- Build relationships with colleagues
- Understand team processes and workflows
- Set performance goals with manager
- Attend company-wide meetings

Month 2-3 - Full Integration:
- Take on more responsibilities
- Contribute to team discussions
- Complete any remaining training
- Participate in performance review
- Identify areas for growth and development

Onboarding Resources:
- Employee Handbook: Available in HR portal
- Training Portal: Access via company intranet
- Mentor Program: Ask HR about mentor assignment
- Employee Resource Groups: Join groups that interest you

Support During Onboarding:
- Your Manager: Primary point of contact for role-specific questions
- HR Team: For benefits, policies, and general questions
- IT Support: For technical issues and access
- Your Mentor: For guidance and advice (if assigned)

What to Expect:
- Lots of information - take notes!
- Multiple meetings and introductions
- Training sessions and modules
- Questions from colleagues getting to know you
- A learning curve - this is normal!

Remember: Onboarding is a process, not a one-day event. Take your time to learn and adjust. We're here to help you succeed!
