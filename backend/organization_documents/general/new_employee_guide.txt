New Employee and Intern Guide

Welcome to the Organization!

This guide is designed to help new employees and interns get started and feel comfortable in their new role.

First Day Checklist:
1. Complete HR onboarding paperwork
2. Set up your computer and email account
3. Meet your team and manager
4. Review your job description and responsibilities
5. Get your employee ID and access cards
6. Familiarize yourself with the office layout

First Week Priorities:
- Understand your role and responsibilities
- Learn about your team and key contacts
- Set up your workspace and tools
- Review company policies and procedures
- Attend orientation sessions
- Ask questions - no question is too small!

Key Contacts:
- HR Department: hr@organization.com, ext. 100
- IT Support: it@organization.com, ext. 200
- Facilities: facilities@organization.com, ext. 300
- Your Manager: Check your welcome email

Getting Started:
1. Review the employee handbook
2. Complete required training modules
3. Set up your calendar and schedule
4. Introduce yourself to colleagues
5. Explore company resources and tools

Common Questions for New Employees:
- Where do I park? Parking is available in the main lot. Get your parking pass from HR.
- What's the dress code? Business casual. When in doubt, dress professionally.
- How do I request time off? Submit requests through the HR portal or speak with your manager.
- Who do I contact for IT issues? Email it@organization.com or call ext. 200.
- What are the lunch options? The office has a cafeteria, and there are many restaurants nearby.

Tips for Success:
- Be proactive and ask questions
- Take notes during meetings and training
- Build relationships with your colleagues
- Set up regular check-ins with your manager
- Take advantage of learning opportunities
- Don't be afraid to make mistakes - that's how we learn!

Remember: Everyone was new once. Don't hesitate to reach out for help or clarification. We're here to support your success!
